CBOS PPM

CBOS PPM or Project Portfolio Management enables executives to have an overall view of of all ongoing demand cases and projects within their organization. PPM provides executives with insights and foresights on the perfomance of their various projects investments.
Key features of CBOS PPM include:
  • Demand management
  • Portfolio ROI Analysis
  • Portfolio Reporting Dashboard
  • Project Resource Database Management
  • Project Phase Exit Management
  • Product Innovation Processs Management

CBOS PMS

CBOS PMS or Project Management System enables project managers to effeciently track project resources, schedule, and cost. Project tem members can also use PMS to provide feeback on their tasks pregress and collaborate with team members.
Key features of CBOS PMS include:
  • Project WBS Development
  • Project Resources Tracking
  • Project Cost Tracking
  • Project Schedule tracking
  • Project Change Request Management
  • Project Risk Management
  • Project Status Reporting

CBOS CRM

CBOS CRM is a flexible customer relationship management supporting marketing, sales, and services management. Companies can futher customize CBOS CRM to suit their specific business process.
Key features of CBOS CRM include:
  • Sales Forecast Management
  • Sales Leads Development
  • Sales Orders Management
  • Product Inventory Management
  • Marketing Campaign Management
  • After Sales Service Management

CBOS DMS

CBOS DMS is a fully customizable document management system that enables online storage and organization of companies documents. Employees can share documents and perform on collaborative documents editing irrespective of their geographical location. CBOS DMS also provides document accesscontrol and documents approval management. CBOS scalable document storage technology provides fast access to documents regardless of the number of documents stored, That is the same speed of access for 10 documents or 10 million documents.
Key features of CBOS DMS include:
  • Scalable Document Storage
  • Document Access Control
  • Document Content Text Search
  • Multiple Document Formats(pdf,word,ppt, excel, xml,json, etc.)
  • Document Version Control

CBOS KMS

CBOS KMS(knowledge management system) is built on top of CBOS DMS. It is built to support the knowledge management process , knowledge generating, knowledge capturing, knowledge transfering, and knowledge re-using.
In addition to the Key features of CBOS DMS, CBOS KMS also include:
  • Knowledge Topology Analysis
  • Multi-dimentional Content Tagging
  • Knowledge Management Process
  • Knowledge Community of Practice
